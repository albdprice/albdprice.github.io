Research Interests

I develop and apply computational methods at the intersection of quantum chemistry, machine learning, and AI-driven materials science. My work focuses on:

Density functional theory — Exchange-hole dipole moment (XDM) dispersion corrections, adaptive hybrid functionals, and method development within the FHI-aims and Psi4 software packages.

Machine learning for molecular science — MACE neural network potentials, physics-informed adaptive parameter learning, and ML-accelerated molecular property prediction.

AI-driven research — Agentic AI systems for drug discovery and materials chemistry, autonomous computational workflows, and MLOps for scientific computing.

Selected Projects
