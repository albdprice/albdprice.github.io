Research Interests

Density functional theory — Exchange-hole dipole moment (XDM) dispersion corrections, adaptive hybrid functionals (aPBE0), local hybrid density functionals, and method development within the FHI-aims and Psi4 packages. Named developer for FHI-aims.

Machine learning for molecular science — MACE neural network potentials, physics-informed adaptive parameter learning for interatomic potentials, and ML-accelerated molecular property prediction.

AI-driven research — Agentic AI systems for drug discovery, autonomous computational workflows, MLOps for scientific computing, and AI-powered materials design.
